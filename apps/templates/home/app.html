HOW TO USE

STEPS TO USE

Step 1:In the dashboard you can navigate to the metaverse page where you can click the button to enter metaverse to consult with doctor/patient.

Step 2:After capturing the session with the patient the user can upload the video in the metaverse page to get the summary of the seesion along with the AI insights.

Step 3:If the you have pre recorded calls they can navigate to summary page to get the summary of the audio and also AI insights.

Step 4:In the perform AI analysis page you can get the insights of the duration of the call, topic discussed, Key words, Key Sentences invloved in the conversation and also satisfaction level of the patient/user involved.

Step 5:If you want to retrieve the past history of a user you can navigate to past summary page and enter your name and phone number.

Step 6:Now you can also see all the Security Policies followed in the Security Policies page

Step 7:If you have any queries related to our application you can navigate to support page.

Step 8:If the feedback page you can provide feedback to help us improve our performance.
